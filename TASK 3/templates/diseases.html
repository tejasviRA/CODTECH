Plant Disease Guide

A comprehensive list of diseases, symptoms, and cures for plant health.

Yellow Curl

Symptoms: Leaves become yellow and curl upward.

Cure: Apply neem oil or sulfur-based fungicides. Improve air circulation around plants.

Mosaic Virus

Symptoms: Leaves show a mottled yellow or white pattern, sometimes with stunted growth.

Cure: Remove infected plants, disinfect tools, and control aphids with insecticidal soap.

Spider Mite

Symptoms: Fine webbing on leaves, yellow speckles, and leaf drop.

Cure: Spray plants with water, use insecticidal soap, or introduce predatory mites.

Septoria Leaf Spot

Symptoms: Small, dark spots with gray centers appear on lower leaves.

Cure: Remove infected leaves and apply copper-based fungicides.

Leaf Mold

Symptoms: Yellow spots on upper leaf surfaces with fuzzy mold underneath.

Cure: Improve air circulation and apply fungicides like potassium bicarbonate.

Late Blight

Symptoms: Water-soaked lesions on leaves and stems, with white mold under leaves.

Cure: Remove and destroy infected plants and apply fungicides.

Early Blight

Symptoms: Circular brown spots with concentric rings on older leaves.

Cure: Use disease-resistant varieties and apply fungicides regularly.

Bacterial Spot

Symptoms: Dark, water-soaked spots on leaves, fruits, and stems.

Cure: Apply copper-based sprays and avoid overhead watering.

Leaf Scorch

Symptoms: Leaves turn brown along edges and may curl inward.

Cure: Ensure consistent watering and avoid over-fertilizing.

Powdery Mildew

Symptoms: White, powdery fungal growth on leaves and stems.

Cure: Apply sulfur or potassium bicarbonate-based fungicides.

Haunglongbing (Citrus Greening)

Symptoms: Yellowing of leaves, misshapen fruits, and tree decline.

Cure: Remove infected trees and control psyllid insects.

Esca (Black Measles)

Symptoms: Black streaks in leaves and fruit discoloration.

Cure: Prune affected vines and maintain good vineyard hygiene.

Black Rot

Symptoms: Black lesions on leaves and shriveled fruits.

Cure: Apply fungicides and remove infected parts of plants.

Common Rust

Symptoms: Reddish-brown pustules on leaves and stems.

Cure: Use resistant varieties and apply fungicides.

Gray Leaf Spot

Symptoms: Narrow, brown lesions on leaves with gray centers.

Cure: Avoid overhead watering and apply fungicides.

Scab

Symptoms: Raised, corky spots on fruits and leaves.

Cure: Use resistant varieties and apply fungicides like mancozeb.
